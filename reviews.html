Delicious Daim

Daim is a popular chocolate candy known for its crunchy almond caramel center coated in smooth milk chocolate.

About Daim

Daim was first introduced in Sweden in 1953 and quickly became a favorite treat worldwide. It is produced by Mondelez International and is available in various sizes and packaging.
